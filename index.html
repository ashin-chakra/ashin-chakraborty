Electoral integrity and democratic participation

Gender, Social Identity, and Voter Deletion: Evidence from the 2026 Special Intensive Revision of Electoral Rolls in West Bengal

A large-scale empirical project examining voter exclusions during the 2026 Special Intensive Revision of electoral rolls across 294 Assembly constituencies and approximately 80,000 polling booths in West Bengal. The work involved digitizing, structuring, and analyzing large-scale Electoral Commission records, including 150M+ individual voter records.

Methods: administrative data digitization, web scraping, data cleaning, machine-readable electoral records, gender/religion/caste classification, constituency- and booth-level analysis.
